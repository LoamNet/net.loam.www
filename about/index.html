About Loam

Who We Are

Loam creates games and apps, and helps indie content creators with resources and publishing to help create their own games, apps, art, and other media. We make money by selling software and physical goods (like dragon ink), by providing paid technology services, and through donations! The goal is less about profit and more about making cool stuff, and using that cool stuff to fund more cool stuff.
