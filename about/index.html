About Loam

Who We Are

Loam an indie digital creator group that not only makes traditional and digital media, but also tries to provide other creators with low cost or free resources and services. We make our money from donations given by our generous supporters who enjoy our free content, and through traditional and digital comissions and services. Our goal is to make cool and help others make cool stuff along the way! For any questions about our goods, services, or mission, feel free to reach out via the contact page.
