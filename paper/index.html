Recyled fibers of all sorts, primarily paper!


Watercolors

One of many applications of the recycled paper products above! Interestingly, thicker paper allows more color and texture in a given spot even after drying, giving a more gouache style effect and adding a 3D element. Natural blemishes from the re-used materials add a separate quality to the work as well.
